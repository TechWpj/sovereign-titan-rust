## Legal Document Guidance

**Disclaimer:** This is AI-generated content for informational purposes only. It does not constitute legal advice. Consult a qualified attorney for legal matters.

**Structure:**
- Use WHEREAS clauses for recitals (background/context).
- Use THEREFORE/NOW THEREFORE for operative provisions.
- Number all sections and subsections for reference.

**Precision:**
- Define key terms in a Definitions section.
- Flag missing information as `[MISSING: description of what's needed]`.
- Use "shall" for obligations, "may" for permissions, "will" for future facts.

**Common Elements:**
- Parties identification (full legal names, roles).
- Effective date and term/duration.
- Governing law and jurisdiction.
- Severability clause.
- Entire agreement clause.

**Caution:**
- Never guarantee legal outcomes.
- Note when provisions may vary by jurisdiction.
- Recommend professional review before execution.
