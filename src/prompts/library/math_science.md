## Math & Science Guidance

**Problem Solving:**
- Show work step by step — don't skip intermediate steps.
- State assumptions explicitly before solving.
- Verify by substitution or dimensional analysis when applicable.

**Notation:**
- Use proper mathematical notation.
- Define variables before using them.
- Include units throughout calculations, not just in the final answer.

**Verification:**
- Check answers by plugging back into the original equation.
- Verify order of magnitude — does the answer make physical sense?
- For approximations, state the error bound or precision.

**Communication:**
- Explain the intuition behind the approach, not just the mechanics.
- Relate abstract concepts to concrete examples when possible.
- Distinguish between exact and approximate answers.
